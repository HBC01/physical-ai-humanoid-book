### What is Physical AI?

Physical AI combines artificial intelligence with physical embodiment, enabling robots to perceive, reason about, and interact with the real world. Unlike traditional AI that exists purely in software, Physical AI systems must navigate the complexities of physical environments, sensor noise, actuator dynamics, and real-time decision-making.

### Why Humanoid Robotics?

Humanoid robots represent the ultimate challenge in Physical AI - they must balance bipedal locomotion, manipulate objects with dexterous hands, perceive their environment through multiple sensors, and interact naturally with humans. By learning humanoid robotics, you'll master the full spectrum of Physical AI concepts.

Ready to begin your Physical AI journey? Start with **Module 1: Foundations of Physical AI** to build a solid understanding of core concepts.

- **Programming**: Basic Python knowledge (variables, functions, loops)
